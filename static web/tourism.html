Tourism places

Taj Mahala

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Vrindavan

Vrindavan, in Uttar Pradesh, India, is a sacred town associated with the childhood and youth of Lord Krishna. It is renowned for its temples, vibrant festivals, and devotion-filled atmosphere.

Araku

Araku is a scenic hill station in Andhra Pradesh, India, known for its lush valleys, waterfalls, and coffee plantations. It is a popular getaway for its pleasant climate and natural beauty in the Eastern Ghats.

Golconda fort

Golconda Fort in Hyderabad, India, is a historic fortress famous for its brilliant acoustics and grand architecture. It was once a flourishing center for the diamond trade, including the famed Koh-i-Noor.
