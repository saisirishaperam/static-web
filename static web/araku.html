Araku Valley

Araku Valley is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Eastern Ghats of Andhra Pradesh, India, known for its cool climate, lush green landscapes, and serene beauty. Surrounded by coffee plantations, dense forests, waterfalls, and tribal villages, it offers a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The valley is also home to the Borra Caves, famous for their stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations. With its scenic train journey through tunnels and bridges, Araku is often called the "Ooty of Andhra Pradesh" and is a popular year-round tourist destination.
